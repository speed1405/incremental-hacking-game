# Feature Suggestions for Incremental Hacking Game üöÄ

This document contains ideas for future enhancements to make the game even more engaging and feature-rich.

## üéØ Gameplay Mechanics

### 1. **Prestige System**
- Reset the game but keep some permanent bonuses
- Gain "Reputation Points" based on highest level and total XP
- Use Reputation to unlock permanent multipliers
- Example: 1 Reputation = +5% permanent power boost

### 2. **Mission Cooldowns & Repeatable Missions**
- Add cooldown timers to missions (e.g., 30 seconds, 1 minute)
- Allow players to repeat missions for consistent XP/Credit farming
- Higher difficulty missions have longer cooldowns but better rewards

### 3. **Skills & Skill Trees**
- Unlock skill points when leveling up
- Skill categories:
  - **Hacking Speed**: Reduce mission cooldowns
  - **Efficiency**: Get more XP/Credits from missions
  - **Automation**: Increase passive power generation
  - **Bargaining**: Reduce hardware costs

### 4. **Achievements System**
- Track player milestones and reward completion
- Examples:
  - "First Hack" - Complete your first mission
  - "Power User" - Reach 1,000 power/sec
  - "Elite Hacker" - Reach Level 50
  - "Billionaire" - Accumulate 1,000,000 credits
- Grant small bonuses for achievements (XP, Credits, or power multipliers)

### 5. **Random Events**
- Periodic random events that affect gameplay
- Examples:
  - "Security Breach" - Lose 10% power but gain 2x XP for next mission
  - "Hardware Sale" - 25% off all hardware for 60 seconds
  - "Bounty Mission" - Limited-time mission with 3x rewards
  - "Server Maintenance" - All missions disabled for 30 seconds

## üí∞ Economy & Resources

### 6. **Multiple Currencies**
- Add "Data" as a third currency from specific missions
- Use Data to unlock special upgrades or access hidden missions
- Add "Reputation" for prestige system

### 7. **Black Market**
- Special shop that appears randomly
- Offers unique, powerful, but expensive items
- Limited-time availability (e.g., 2 minutes)

### 8. **Dynamic Pricing**
- Hardware costs increase based on how many you own
- Mission rewards scale with player level

## üîß Hardware & Upgrades

### 9. **Hardware Sets & Synergies**
- Owning specific combinations gives bonuses
- Example: "Intel CPU + NVIDIA GPU" = +10% power bonus
- "Full AMD Build" = +15% to all generation

### 10. **Hardware Upgrades**
- Upgrade existing hardware instead of just buying new ones
- Example: Overclock CPU for +25% power at +50% cost

### 11. **Special Hardware**
- Quantum Computers (extremely expensive, massive power boost)
- AI Assistants (generate Credits passively)
- Network Cards (reduce mission power costs)
- Cooling Systems (allow overclocking)

## üéÆ User Experience

### 12. **Statistics & Analytics**
- Add a "Stats" tab showing:
  - Total missions completed
  - Total XP earned
  - Total Credits spent
  - Time played
  - Most used hardware
  - Level up history

### 13. **Themes & Customization**
- Different color schemes (Matrix green, Cyberpunk blue, Hacker dark)
- Toggle animations on/off
- Sound effects and background music (optional)

### 14. **Tooltips & Help System**
- Hover tooltips explaining features
- Tutorial for first-time players
- Help button with game mechanics explanation

### 15. **Offline Progress**
- Calculate power generation while player was away
- Cap offline gains (e.g., max 8 hours)
- Show "Welcome back" notification with offline earnings

## üèÜ Competitive & Social

### 16. **Leaderboards** (if adding backend)
- Global leaderboard for highest level
- Speed run categories (fastest to level 50)
- Highest power/sec achieved

### 17. **Challenges & Daily Quests**
- Daily challenges with special rewards
- Examples:
  - "Complete 10 missions in one session"
  - "Reach 500 power/sec"
  - "Level up 3 times in one day"

### 18. **Hacker Titles**
- Earn titles based on achievements
- Display title next to player level
- Examples:
  - "Script Kiddie" (Level 1-5)
  - "White Hat" (Level 6-15)
  - "Elite Hacker" (Level 16-30)
  - "Cyber Legend" (Level 31+)

## üé® Visual Enhancements

### 19. **Animations & Effects**
- Particle effects when completing missions
- Glowing effects on available missions
- Screen shake on level up
- Floating XP/Credit numbers when earning

### 20. **Progress Visualization**
- Charts showing power generation over time
- Mission completion history graph
- Level progression timeline

## üîê Advanced Missions

### 21. **Mission Chains**
- Multi-step missions that unlock sequentially
- Example: "Corporate Heist" chain:
  1. Recon the network
  2. Steal credentials
  3. Infiltrate servers
  4. Extract data
  5. Cover tracks
- Each step unlocks the next, final step gives big reward

### 22. **Elite Missions**
- Very difficult missions requiring high level AND high power
- Extremely high rewards
- Maybe require specific hardware combinations

### 23. **Timed Missions**
- Missions that must be completed within a time limit
- Higher risk, higher reward
- Failure results in losing some power or credits

## üåê Multiplayer Ideas (Advanced)

### 24. **Cooperative Missions**
- Team up with other players to complete hard missions
- Share rewards among participants

### 25. **PvP Hacking Battles**
- Challenge other players
- Winner takes credits/power from loser
- Protected mode for players who don't want PvP

## üîÆ Endgame Content

### 26. **Infinite Scaling**
- Missions that scale infinitely with player level
- Endless progression for hardcore players

### 27. **Secret Missions**
- Hidden missions unlocked by specific conditions
- Easter eggs and references to hacker culture
- Extremely difficult with unique rewards

### 28. **New Game+ Mode**
- Complete the game to unlock NG+
- Enemies are harder, rewards are better
- Unlock exclusive hardware and missions

## üì± Technical Improvements

### 29. **Export/Import Save**
- Allow players to export save as JSON
- Import save on different devices
- Backup functionality

### 30. **Performance Optimizations**
- Reduce DOM updates for better performance
- Virtual scrolling for long hardware lists
- Optimize game loop
